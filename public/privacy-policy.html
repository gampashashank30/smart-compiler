2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the SmartCompiler platform
  • Authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • Process your C code through our compilation and AI analysis systems
  • Track your learning progress, bug history, and compilation statistics
  • Serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense (with your consent where required)

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not share your code submissions with anyone outside of our AI processing pipeline (Anthropic Claude API for analysis).

5 GDPR — Rights for EU/EEA Users

If you are located in the European Union, European Economic Area, or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to our processing of your data for direct marketing or profiling.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw any consent you've given at any time — including for advertising cookies — via our cookie settings button.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract Performance: To provide you the SmartCompiler service you've signed up for.
  • Legitimate Interests: For analytics, security, and site improvement.
  • Consent: For advertising cookies and marketing communications.
  • Legal Obligation: Where required by law.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at: privacy@maadiotsolutions.co.in. We will respond within 30 days as required by GDPR.

6 CCPA — Rights for California Users

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) / CPRA grants you additional rights:

  • Right to Know: What categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect, use, disclose, or sell.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information (as defined by CCPA, this includes sharing data with ad networks for cross-context behavioral advertising).
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

We participate in interest-based advertising which may constitute a "sale" or "sharing" of data under CCPA. To opt out, you can:

  • Click the "Cookie Settings" button at the bottom of our site
  • Visit Google's Ad Center to opt out of personalized ads
  • Enable Restricted Data Processing by contacting us at privacy@maadiotsolutions.co.in

7 Data Retention

9 Children's Privacy

SmartCompiler is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the EEA/UK).

If we discover we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us with personal information should contact us at privacy@maadiotsolutions.co.in.

⚠️ Google AdSense does not serve personalized ads to users under 13. Our site is intended for CS students, typically aged 16+.

10 Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Supabase Row-Level Security (RLS) ensures users can only access their own data
  • WebSocket connections secured with short-lived authentication tickets
  • No passwords stored — we use Google OAuth only
  • Server logs reviewed for anomalous access patterns

Despite these measures, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
